2. Purpose

The parties wish to establish a partnership whereby Roofwander promotes the Brand's rooftop tent products and facilitates rentals through its marketplace (roofwander.com); the Brand promotes Roofwander to its customers, owners and audience. Both parties benefit from increased visibility, referrals, and optional integration services as described below.

3. Partnership Options

The parties may agree on one or more of the following cooperation models:

  • Performance Partnership: Roofwander provides a trackable link. The Brand shares it via newsletter, website, blog or social media. Commission structure as agreed in Appendix or separate addendum.
  • SEO & Content: Roofwander publishes brand features (blog, website page) with links to the Brand. The Brand may be listed as an official partner with logo and backlink. In exchange, the Brand provides a mention and link to Roofwander on its website, partners page, blog or newsletter.
  • Map Integration: Roofwander provides a JSON feed of owners listing the Brand's tents. The Brand may embed a "Find a tent near you" map on its website. Terms and technical details to be agreed separately.

Specific commercial terms (e.g. commission per listing, per booking) shall be documented in an addendum or separate commercial agreement.

4. Obligations

  • Roofwander undertakes to operate the marketplace in good faith, provide agreed tracking, content and/or integration services, promote the Brand where agreed, and compensate the Brand according to agreed terms.
  • Brand undertakes to promote Roofwander to its customers and audience (e.g. via trackable links, website mention, newsletter, partners page), to represent Roofwander fairly, and to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

5. Term & Termination

This agreement is effective from and remains in force until terminated. Either party may terminate with days' written notice. Termination does not affect accrued rights or obligations.
