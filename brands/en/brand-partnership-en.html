Roofwander Brand Partnership Program

Three simple ways to collaborate — starting with sharing a trackable link, then expanding to content/SEO and optional map integration.

Who We Are

Roofwander is a rooftop tent rental marketplace connecting owners and renters. We help owners offset the cost of their tent by renting it out, while making rooftop tent adventures more accessible.

Partnership Options

1) Performance Partnership (Trackable Link)

We provide a unique tracking link you can share via newsletter, website, blog or social. You earn commission on measurable actions.

Example structure:

  • €20 per owner listing
  • €20 per completed booking
Flexible structure depending on your goals and audience.
2) SEO + Content (+ Social if you want)

We publish a dedicated brand feature (blog and/or website page) with links back to you. We can also add your logo as an official partner on our website (with backlink).

In exchange, we'd love a mention + link to Roofwander on your website (partners page, blog, or newsletter).

Examples: Blog • Website
3) Live Owner Map Integration (Your Brand Only)

We provide a lightweight JSON feed showing owners who list your brand tents on Roofwander. Your team can embed a "Find a tent near you" map on your website.

Filter by brand, region, or models. Easy developer integration.
