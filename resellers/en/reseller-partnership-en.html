Who We Are

Roofwander is a rooftop tent rental marketplace connecting owners and renters. We help owners offset the cost of their tent by renting it when they are not using it, while making rooftop tent adventures more accessible.

Partnership Options

1) Performance Partnership (Trackable Link)

Our goal is simple: bring more rooftop tent owners onto Roofwander. If you have owners in your customer base or newsletter, send them via a trackable link.

Example structure:

  • €20 per owner listing
  • €20 per completed booking

If someone contacts your store asking to rent a tent, redirect them to Roofwander and earn €20 when a booking happens.

Flexible and negotiable. Works both ways: owners can reimburse their tent by renting, renters can try before buying.
2) Visibility & Collaboration (SEO / Content)

We can feature your store on Roofwander to boost visibility and strengthen SEO for both partners.
