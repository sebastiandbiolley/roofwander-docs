2. Purpose

The parties wish to establish a mutual partnership: Roofwander promotes the Reseller's store on its marketplace and facilitates rentals through roofwander.com; the Reseller promotes Roofwander to its customers (owners and renters), redirecting them to Roofwander when they want to rent a rooftop tent or try before buying. Both parties benefit from increased visibility and revenue opportunities.

3. Partnership Options

The parties may agree on one or more of the following cooperation models:

  • Performance Partnership: Roofwander provides a trackable link. The Reseller shares it with owners in its customer base or newsletter, or redirects renters to Roofwander. Commission structure (e.g. per owner listing, per completed booking) as agreed in Appendix or separate addendum.
  • Visibility & Collaboration (SEO / Content): Roofwander features the Reseller's store on Roofwander (partner mention, logo + backlink). Roofwander may include the Reseller in blog articles or guides. In exchange, the Reseller provides a mention and link to Roofwander on its website, partners page, blog or newsletter.

Specific commercial terms (e.g. commission per listing, per booking) shall be documented in an addendum or separate commercial agreement.

4. Obligations

  • Roofwander undertakes to operate the marketplace in good faith, provide agreed tracking and/or content services, promote the Reseller where agreed, and compensate the Reseller according to agreed terms.
  • Reseller undertakes to promote Roofwander to its customers (e.g. via trackable links, website mention, newsletter), to represent Roofwander fairly, and to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

5. Term & Termination

This agreement is effective from and remains in force until terminated. Either party may terminate with days' written notice. Termination does not affect accrued rights or obligations.
